# MD Value Check
Web Browser Version: 
https://lprkc.github.io/MD-VALUECHECK/
> A compensation analysis tool that helps physicians evaluate whether their employment offers are equitable and aligned with market benchmarks.

## 📋 Overview

MD Value Check transforms opaque physician compensation packages into clear, data-driven insights. By comparing offers against geographically-adjusted specialty benchmarks (MGMA/SullivanCotter), the tool reveals the full financial picture — from take-home pay to the hospital's total investment in the physician.

## ✨ Features

- **🌎 Geographic Market Adjustment** — Automatically adjusts national median compensation for regional cost-of-labor variations across major markets (NYC, San Francisco, Chicago, Dallas, Miami, and more).
- **📊 Specialty Benchmarking** — Enter your specialty's national median to generate a geo-adjusted target compensation figure tailored to your market.
- **💰 Complete Cash Offer Analysis** — Captures every component of physician compensation: base salary, sign-on bonus, wRVU productivity bonuses, weekly hours, and additional stipends or call pay.
- **🏥 Total Cost of Employment Calculator** — Reveals the "gap" between take-home pay and the employer's total outlay by factoring in benefits, malpractice + tail coverage, and employer-side taxes (FICA, etc.).
- **⚖️ Fairness Score** — Generates an at-a-glance verdict on whether an offer is below, at, or above fair market value, with a clear market variance percentage.
- **⏱️ Effective Hourly Rate** — Cuts through complex compensation structures to show what the offer truly pays per hour worked.
- **📄 PDF Report Generation** — Export a professional summary to share with advisors, attorneys, or use during contract negotiations.

## 🛠️ Usage

1. Select your geographic market from the dropdown
2. Enter the national median compensation for your specialty
3. Input the components of your cash offer (base, bonuses, hours, etc.)
4. Add the employer's cost of benefits, malpractice, and taxes
5. Review your Fairness Score and Market Variance
6. Export a PDF report for your records or negotiations

## 📈 Data Sources

Benchmarks reference industry-standard compensation surveys including MGMA (Medical Group Management Association) and SullivanCotter.
